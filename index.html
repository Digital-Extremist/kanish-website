Frequently Asked Questions

For Mechanics

How do I request parts?

You can request parts by entering your vehicle number and selecting the relevant categories. You can then describe the parts needed via text, voice note, or image upload—whichever is convenient.

How do I get quotes?

Once your request is submitted, verified suppliers matching your criteria will respond with quotes. You can review and compare them before proceeding.

Are all parts available instantly?

Availability depends on supplier stock and region. Commonly used parts are prioritized for quick fulfillment, while specialty items may take longer to source.

Is there a delivery service?

Yes, delivery coordination is supported in select areas. Timelines and fees vary depending on the supplier's terms and distance.

Can I talk to a supplier directly?

For transparency and efficiency, all communication initially happens through the app. Direct contact is facilitated post-confirmation if required.

For Suppliers

How do I start receiving mechanic requests?

Register as a supplier, share your category preferences, and we'll match you with incoming part requests aligned to your offerings.

Do I need to list every part I sell?

No detailed listings are required. You only need to define the brands and categories you can supply—we handle the matching.

How do I respond to mechanic requests?

You'll receive requests in your supplier dashboard. Simply respond with pricing, availability, and delivery estimates.

Is there any charge to receive leads?

Lead access is subject to your subscription plan. Basic plans may include limited visibility, while premium plans unlock priority leads and visibility.

Can I expand into new districts or states?

Yes, suppliers can choose their coverage areas and scale regionally or nationally based on their capacity.
